How it works

  1. Enter any UK company number as the seed (e.g. a group's holding company)
  2. The app finds all current officers/members of that company
  3. For each person it discovers every UK company they're linked to
  4. It downloads the latest balance sheet PDF from Companies House and reads the numbers
  5. Data is cached — first load takes 1–3 minutes, after that it's instant
  6. New companies appear automatically on the next hourly refresh
